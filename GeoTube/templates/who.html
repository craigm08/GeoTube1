Who are motovloggers?

Meet some of the cream of the crop. Whether big time or small, these motovloggers have kept up a standard on YouTube like no other.


Downshift83

Master of stand-up wheelies and creator of The R3sistance, Downshift83 is definitely one to follow if you have a more relaxed view on life. Downshift spares no one when it comes to language so consider yourself warned. He's the owner of two amazing machines Vanessa the Yamaha R1 and Synthia the Ducati Diavel, so if you're into beautiful bikes and Florida sunshine, he's your guy.

Watch

ChaseOnTwoWheels

A well-known Georgia vlogger, Chase is based out of Atlanta, Ga. Currently riding for team Yamaha on an R6 and WR250X, Chase provides viewers with a different experience with a myriad of accents. Known for his Ducati Panigale review, R6 history, and inner city routes, viewers will find themselves playing in traffic more than most other vloggers; lane splits abound.

Watch

Mizshift

Unofficially crowned the King of Editing, Mizshift brings motovlogging and DSLR editing to a whole new level. Originally beginning with cinematic style videos, then transitioning into traditional vlogs, Mizshift has an uncanny ability to make cinematic style videos fall from the wood-work. Hailing from Illinois, expect to see a decent bit of snow. Oh, and wheelies.

Watch

Adjriannicelodean

One of Australias best, Adjrian brings Sydney, Australia to life in his videos. Known for riding an R1 and consistently ranting at cagers, Adjrian keeps things as real as it gets. From topics like motorcycle safety to interacting with subscribers, Adjrians content can really have an impact on his viewers. Although uploads are sparse, keep your eyes to his channel on the reg.

Watch

YummiR6

Known as motovloggings wild child, YummiR6 hails from Virginia Beach, Virginia. Obviously riding an R6, he also totes the keys to a WR250X named Chloe. Viewers can sometimes find themselves lost in his exhaust notes as he goes on tangents in between Fan Mail Fridays and post office problems. Definitely hit his channel for regular scheduled videos and new content every other day.

Watch

Stkrbombd

One of Georgia's up-and-comers, Stkrbombd sports a Suzuki GS500F named Tessa that has appropriately been sticker bombed. Known for his old bike Ruth, a Ninja 500 street fighter, Stkrbombd has had one hell of a start. His video uploads tend to be rather sporadic but usually come from the heart. Expect to see a lot more from this guy in the next year.

Watch

Witness2282

Another R1 owner down in sunny Miami, Florida, Witness is one of the few vloggers that creates quality videos despite having large gaps in upload times. Another participant of Variant Nation, Witness rocks a red Variant and an amazing audio setup. If you're a stickler for various angles, great audio, and in depth vlogs, Witness2282 is who your looking for. Just don't bash nurses... You may get hurt.

Watch

BrianS1K

Another Georgia vlogger, BrianS1K sports a "smexy" BMW S1000RR. Towering in at 6'8", he's one of the few guys that actually fits a liter bike, let alone rides it like it's supposed to. You can expect to see him ride around Braselton, Georgia and a decent bit of Blood Mountain in his videos. He's been on a recent hiatus, but he's come back in style with an awesome sticker and amazing upgrades to his ride.

Watch

Iamramekin

Riding a Yamaha FZ-09, Rammy is best known for his adventures with ChaseOnTwoWheels. With various Blood Mountain vlogs under his belt, Rammy is no stranger to running long rides out north for hours on end. After having spent a good portion of the last few years developing guides and helping folks lose weight across YouTube, Ramekin is just one down to earth dude.

Watch

YourPalsChrisAndAl

Want some two for one goodness? Que YourPalsChrisAndAl. These two Aussies are no stranger to getting wild. With numerous "wrecks" between them and their supermotos and smaller sport bikes, they lean more towards the humorous side of vlogging than most everyone else. It takes a bit to get used to their quick banter back and forth, but the dual cameras is well worth the wait.

Watch

ShagInTheWind

Known for his antics with Mrs. Shaginthewind, Shag is one hell of a guy. Repping metro Atlanta, Shaginthewind rides a murdered out CBR1000RR Fireblade With sporadic but well thought out content, his channel is one to keep your eye on. Although he doesn't have a specific upload schedule, you can expect to hear awesome vlogs from this dude. Oh, and don't forget to mention the Mrs.

Watch

ZJ Motovlogs

A night shift dude by occupation, ZJ does quite a bit of his vlogs in the wee hours of the morning. Creator of Married Vlog Mondays, he and his wife give couples content they can relate to. ZJ tears up the streets on a beautiful 636 named Pheonix, (AKA Lady Bug) which is nearly unmistakable around Boston. Definitely subscirbe if you're into keeping your feed busy on a regular basis.
